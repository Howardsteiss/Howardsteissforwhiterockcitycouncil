About Me

My name is Howard Steiss, and I am seeking to fill the vacant seat on White Rock City Council. Having lived in this beautiful city for 4 years in the uptown neighborhood, I am deeply invested in its future and believe my common sense approach to making lives better, and problem solving can bring a fresh and pragmatic perspective to our city council.

I'm running because I believe in proactive community engagement and sustainable development that respects White Rock's unique character. Our city faces important challenges regarding housing, revitalizing our local business areas, crime prevention and ensuring our infrastructure supports our growing population. I am committed to working collaboratively with residents, businesses, and my fellow council members to find balanced solutions that benefit everyone.
